Our Story

Discover Rock Lane

Built in 1964 as Rock Lane Lodge, we were one of the first resorts on the shores of Table Rock Lake. What started as a comfortable lakeside getaway has grown into a full-service resort and marina without ever losing the laid-back, on-the-water spirit that put us on the map.

Today, Rock Lane is where families make memories, boaters find home, and every summer evening ends with live music, cold drinks at the swim-up bar, and a Table Rock Lake sunset.
