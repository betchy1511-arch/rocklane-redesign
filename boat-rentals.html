Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

How much does it cost to rent a pontoon on Table Rock Lake?

Pontoon rental rates on Table Rock Lake range based on size, horsepower, and rental duration. For real-time pricing on every boat in our fleet, visit rentals.rocklane.com or call (417) 338-4433.

Do you need a boating license to rent a boat in Missouri?

Adults born before January 1, 1984, do not need a license. Those born on or after must complete a free Missouri Boater Safety Course. We'll help you verify before your rental.

Can I rent for just a few hours?

Yes. We offer half-day rentals (5 hours) in both morning and afternoon blocks, plus full-day rentals.

What happens if the weather is bad?

Safety first. If conditions are unsafe, we'll work with you to reschedule or refund per our rental agreement. Light rain typically doesn't cancel a rental.

Are pets allowed on rental boats?

Well-behaved dogs are welcome on most rentals with prior approval. Please ask when booking.

Where do I pick up my rental boat?

All rentals begin at the Rock Lane Marina dock, 611 Rock Lane, Branson, MO 65616. Check-in is at the marina office 30 minutes before your rental start time.
